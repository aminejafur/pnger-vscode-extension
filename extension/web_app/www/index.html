PNGER

Configuration

To allow PNGER from accessing your camera please click on the link bellow. Why should i do this ?
chrome://flags/#unsafely-treat-insecure-origin-as-secure.

Take your picture

Press the (+) bellow icon to take your shot, then choose your background removal method.
(Make sure you provided an API in the extension settings)

Save

Once your background is succefully removed, a modal will show asking for saving destination and image name.

PNGER is an extension to make images integration and background removal easier than ever!

It started as a Weekend Project to write my first VScode extension and understand the concepts for building extensions, It's idea is to make it easier for designers to integrate images directly from their cameras to their projects with a background removal feature.

Find more about this extension in my Github.
